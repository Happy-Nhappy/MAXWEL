About

Community Citizen Service is an organization that aims to develop the social, health and academic life of communities to ensure all communities have equal developmental opportunities. The organization has embarked on numerous activities and seek to break more bounds.

Our Mission

Work collaboratively with community members, stakeholders and partners to identify and address the needs and aspirations of communities. Aim at fostering social, economic and environmental progress by implementing strategies and initiatives that promote community well-being, social justice, and equitable development.

Our Vision

To create empowered, sustainable and all inclusive communities where individuals have equal opportunities to thrive and lead fulfilling lives. We envision communities that are vibrant, resilient and supportive with strong social connections ans sense of belonging among residents.

What we can do

We organize summits for students in all levels of education.

Education summit is designed to empower students with an opportunity to engage with leading educators. Moreover, it is organized to bring together the appropraite stakeholders to develop a high level strategy and/or address a crucial, over arching problem.

Education helps people to become better citizens, get a better-paid job, shows the difference between good and bad. Education show us the importance of hardwork, and at the same time, help us grow and develop.

Education is the key to turn a weakness into a strength. It offers different tools and ways to understand problems that lay ahead of us and helps resolve them. More importantly, education provides us with considerable mental agility to make the right decisions and spring into action when needed. Many types of research show that educated women can more easily stand up against gender bias and marital violence as they have improved their decision-making capabilities.
